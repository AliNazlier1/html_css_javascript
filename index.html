About the Song

"Perfect" by Ed Sheeran is a romantic ballad that was released in 2017. The song is a heartfelt dedication to his wife Cherry Seaborn, expressing his deep love and affection. It has become one of Ed Sheeran's most beloved songs, touching hearts worldwide.
